Paris Hilton is engaged for a FOURTH time! Boyfriend Carter Reum, 40, gets down on one knee to propose to socialite on her 40th birthday with HUGE emerald-cut diamond after one year of dating

By Heidi Parker For Dailymail.com 14:27 17 Feb 2021, updated 17:13 17 Feb 2021

  • The 40-year-old reality star is engaged to her venture capitalist boyfriend Carter Reum, 40
  • This marks the fourth time the hotel heiress has been engaged
  • She has previously been proposed to by model Jason Shaw, Greek socialite Paris Latsis, and actor Chris Zylka
  • Paris showed off her emerald cut engagement ring by Jean Dousset, the great-grandson of Louis Cartier
  • Reum, who attended Columbia University, became an entrepreneur when he co-founded alcohol brand VEEV Spirits and also runs M13, an investment company

Paris Hilton has become engaged to her venture capitalist boyfriend of just over one year, Carter Reum, she announced on Wednesday, which also marked her 40th birthday.

The hotel heiress turned reality star shared intimate images from the romantic proposal, which saw the pair both wear white ensembles, as Carter, also 40, got down on one knee after whisking her off to a private island.

ADVERTISEMENT

Paris raised her left hand to show off her emerald cut engagement ring by Jean Dousset, the great-grandson of Louis Cartier.

This is the fourth time that the star has been engaged, having previously been proposed to by model Jason Shaw in 2002, Greek socialite Paris Latsis in 2005, and actor Chris Zylka in 2018.

'When you find your soulmate, you don't just know it. You feel it,' Paris captioned her Instagram announcement, that showed the two on a beach at sunset with light clouds in the background.

The Simple Life star added, 'My love & I have been together since our first date, and for my birthday, he arranged a special trip to tropical paradise.'

Reum, who attended Columbia University, became an entrepreneur when he co-founded VEEV Spirits with brother Courtney, and also runs M13, an investment company. Paris started dating Carter in late 2019 after being friends for 15 years.

Meanwhile, the celebrity DJ shared further details of her romantic proposal, revealing: 'As we walked to dinner along the beach, Carter led us to a cabana adorned with flowers and dropped to one knee.

'I said yes, yes to forever ❤️ There’s no one I’d rather spend forever with. ✨.' She finished with: 'Here's to Love - the Forever Kind.'

In the photos Carter can be seen down on one knee with the ring box open. He then pulls her into his arms and kisses his future wife as she wears sunglasses.

On Paris' website she shared 23 images from the proposal which looked to be a well planned event.

Paris was seen in her plunging dress which had a high slit that showed off her legs. And Carter was dapper in a suit with a white shirt and no tie.

They were seen on the beach as well as in a wood-framed cabana that said Marry Me in cursive on top before walking on the beach.

Also present was Paris' sister Nicky Hilton and her husband of six years, James Rithschild. Brother Barron Hilton II was there as well and he had by his side his wife of almost three years, Tessa Gräfin von Walderdorff.

And there were several members of Reum's family including his mother Sherry Reum as well as his brother Courtney Reum.

ADVERTISEMENT

'I'm excited for our next chapter,' she told People.

Reum proposed on February 13 during a vacation on a private island while celebrating Hilton's 40th birthday.

According to People, she wore a Retrofete dress and a Loschy crown.

Paris also shared with People: 'This past year with COVID has accelerated so many things. As someone who traveled constantly, I had a chance to stay home and reevaluate what was important to me. My relationship and the time I spent with Carter was a gift.

'I'm excited for our next chapter.'

Her fiance Reum then said: 'I have gotten the unique opportunity to get to know the real Paris on a daily basis just the two of us during these past 15 months and I couldn't be more excited and lucky to have her as my future wife and partner.

'She shines with her kindness, work ethic, authenticity and her voice in making the world a better place and she will do the same as a future mother and wife.'

Vogue shared on their Instagram page at 6 am: '@parishilton is engaged to @carterreum_official! Tap the link in our bio for an exclusive look at Reum’s proposal–and the emerald-cut ring.' They also shared a photo of the niece of Kyle Richards with Reum.

'Paris is so excited, and her friends and family are just thrilled for her.

'Carter is a great guy and after everything she's been through, the stable kind of love he brings to her life is what she deserves.

'She's sure he's the one,' a source told People.

Hilton previously revealed that it was Carter's sister, Halle Hammond, that invited her to spend Thanksgiving with her family in 2019; that is when she felt a connection to Carter.

'We just had this incredible chemistry,' said the perfume mogul. 'We had our first date and haven’t spent a night apart since. It’s pretty amazing.'

In January they announced that they were planning a future together and even discussing having children, and they shared they are using IVF to make sure they have twins. The cover girl wants a boy and a girl. She added pal Kim Kardashian told her about the process of gender selection.

Paris has been engaged before. Her first engagement was to model Jason Shaw in the early 2000s then she said yes to a proposal from movie producer Paris Latsis in 2004. In 2018 Hilton flashed a massive 20 carat diamond engagement ring when she became engaged to Leftovers actor Chris Zylka.

This proposal comes days after Hilton starred with Carter in a new music video for her 2006 song Heartbeat. The star revealed to Paper on Friday that she was inspired to revisit the song amid her romance with the businessman.

'I’ve been listening to [her sole studio album Paris] so much with my boyfriend. In 2006, I didn’t even know what love was, but the words to [Heartbeat] finally have meaning,' she revealed.

'My boyfriend is not the type of guy who will be in a music video,' Paris said of Carter's brief appearance.

'He's a businessman and hates the spotlight. He's not that type of person at all. I asked Carter, "I know you'd never do this, but it'd mean so much to me. We'll just shoot you from the back. There's obviously no one I'd rather do this with, the song was literally meant for me and you." He said, "Yes," and I was so happy,' she recalled.
