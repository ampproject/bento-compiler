Details

Outfit your kitchen with the elegant design and superb performance of the Calphalon Premier Stainless Steel Cookware Set. Its 3-layer metal construction features a heavy-gauge aluminum core to quickly and evenly distribute heat on any cooktop.
  • Delivers precise cooking performance, with an elegant, classic look that will inspire any chef
  • 11-piece set includes:
    • 1.5 qt. saucepan with lid
    • 2.5 qt. saucepan with lid
    • 3 qt. saute pan with lid
    • 8 qt. stock pot with lid
    • 10" fry pan
    • 12" fry pan with lid
  • Fully-clad, 3-layer metal construction features a heavy-gauge aluminum core that distributes heat evenly and allows you to brown, sear, and sauté with consistency and confidence
  • Compatible with all stove types, including induction
  • Covered cookware features a tempered glass lid that allows you to monitor cooking progress without letting heat and moisture escape
  • Brushed stainless steel finish
  • Stainless steel handles stay cool to the touch on the stovetop
